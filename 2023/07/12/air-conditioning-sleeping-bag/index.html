air-conditioning sleeping bag

Air conditioning sleeping bag creativity

General introduction

Using semiconductor refrigeration components to replace traditional compressors and refrigerants, achieving the goal of small size and low electricity consumption. So as to make two refrigeration devices. Install these two refrigeration devices in a small box to create air conditioning, and send the air conditioning into the sleeping bag through a pipe. The final air outlet is 4 degrees lower than the ambient temperature, and the plan is feasible

Inspiration

During an extracurricular practice activity organized by a learning organization, I slept in a sleeping bag. Although there was air conditioning in the dormitory, I still felt very stuffy and my feet were very hot , So I want to design such an air-conditioned sleeping bag. In addition, sleeping bag air conditioners are more energy-efficient, environmentally friendly, small in size, and easy to carry than air conditioners. This design is very practical for camping tents , Because the power consumption is low, you can use a mobile power supply

Some details of production process

1.When discussing the connection between the refrigeration plate and the thermal and cooling blocks, applying silicone can increase the contact area, thereby improving the conduction efficiency

  1. I think the most difficult part is not assembling the refrigeration module, but designing the box. If the box is well designed, it can better reduce the temperature. According to Bernoulli’s law, connecting pipes on both sides of a box with a small cross-sectional area can transport gas faster. In addition, the selection of pipes and fans is also worth considering.

  2. For this design, if it is winter, perhaps the refrigeration device can be placed over and installed, with the heat dissipation end facing towards the inside of the box to create heating
